Delifresh at the International Food Expo

Delifresh proudly participated in the International Food Expo 2025, one of the most prestigious events in the food industry. Our booth attracted hundreds of visitors including chefs, hotel managers, and catering businesses looking for high-quality food solutions.

The event was a great opportunity to showcase our premium catering services, highlighting our commitment to freshness, quality, and innovation. From ready-to-serve meals to tailored B2B deals, our solutions captured the attention of key industry leaders.

Our team also networked with international partners, opening the door for new collaborations across hospitality, events, and retail sectors. The feedback received was overwhelmingly positive, reinforcing Delifresh’s position as a trusted catering partner.

"Being part of the International Food Expo allowed us to connect with global leaders and showcase how Delifresh is redefining catering standards."

As we move forward, Delifresh is excited to continue building strong partnerships, offering innovative catering experiences, and expanding our presence in the global food industry. Stay tuned for upcoming events where we’ll keep serving quality and excellence.

Missed us at the Expo? Don’t worry! You can always connect with our team through the Contact Page to learn more about our catering solutions and partnership opportunities.
